Is Therapy Right for You?

Psychotherapy consists of one-on-one client to therapist sessions, typically over a period of 50 minutes per meeting. Depending on the emotional wellbeing of the client, he or she may meet over periods ranging from once a week to one or two times a month until the causes of distress are resolved.
